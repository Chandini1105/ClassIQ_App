Booking a Classroom

Step-by-step guide to reserve a classroom

  1. Navigate to "Book Classroom" from the sidebar
  2. Select your course and faculty
  3. Choose your preferred classroom and date
  4. Set the start time and duration
  5. Optionally assign a student leader
  6. Click "Book" to confirm the reservation

Troubleshooting

Common issues and solutions

  • Can't Find a Room? Check if all classrooms are booked. Try different time slots
  • Booking Failed? Ensure no overlapping time slots. Check date format
  • Missing Faculty? Contact administrator to add faculty members
  • Page Not Loading? Clear browser cache and refresh
  • Time Zone Issues? All times displayed in IST (Indian Standard Time)

Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì Can I book a classroom for multiple days?
Currently, bookings are made on a per-day basis. For recurring classes, you'll need to book each session individually.
‚ùì What's the maximum class duration?
You can book classrooms for any duration, but it cannot extend beyond college closing time. Always check the "College Hours" on the dashboard.
‚ùì Can I modify a booking after creating it?
You'll need to contact the administrator to modify existing bookings. Plan carefully before booking.
‚ùì How do I assign a student leader?
When booking or managing a class, you can select a student leader from the available list. This helps identify who's managing the session.
‚ùì What happens if there's a scheduling conflict?
The system prevents overlapping bookings in the same classroom. If you get a conflict error, choose a different time or classroom.
‚ùì How often is the availability updated?
Classroom availability updates in real-time. Refresh the page to see the latest status.
